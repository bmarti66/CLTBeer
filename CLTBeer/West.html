Blue Blaze Brewing
Located on South Turner Avenue. Blue Blaze is a spacious location that pours excellent craft beer for its' customers. One of the best breweries in Charlotte. Amazing beer that allows dogs onto the premises. Great vibe that makes the beer taste that much better. There is not a ton of variety, but do not let that distract you from the uniqueness of this brewery. The employees are friendly and willing to help with whatever is needed.
