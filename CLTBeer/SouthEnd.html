South End of Charlotte



Sycamore

Sycamore Brewing Company
Extremely popular brewery located on Hawkins Street. Hosts Food Truck Friday every Friday where there are several food trucks selling affordable food to the patrons. There is even live music at this event along with others. Great selection of specifically IPAs and Blondes. It is so popular that often times it feels too crowded inside. The actually brewery is not too large, but they make up for it with the amazing beer. Grab a date and head to Food Truck Friday for a great time!

Directions

Lenny Boy

Lenny Boy Brewing Company
Located on South Tryon Street. Spacious brewery that is kind of tucked away alongside the street. This brewery has been around for decades, and it certainly does not dissapoint. The beer selection is not as large as other breweries but it is quality over quantity. Very laid back atmosphere that any person would enjoy. This brewery has a good sour beer selection, and the staff is very knowledgeable and friendly.

Directions

Triple C

Triple C Brewing Company
Located on Griffith Street. Triple C is a dog-friendly pub that offers a decent variety of beers in its' cozy atmosphere. Outside there is a patio that comes complete with games for the casual drinker. The music is superb and they host multiple events such as yoga, and there is usually a food truck outside. Great service and a wonderful place to meet new people.

Directions

Hyde

Hyde Brewing
Located at the New Bern light rail stop. Combined with the Suffolk Punch cafe, so there's a large selection of food to go along with Dan Hyde's wonderful craft beers. Constantly changing selection that is as vast as it is plentiful. Ecclectic tastes will love this spot as there is many different kinds of drinks to be tried. This great atmosphere is open everyday and it even offers tours of parts of the brewery.

Directions

Unknown

The Unknown Brewing Company
Located on South Mint Street. This local brewery offers an industrial style taproom as well as occasional food truck visits. The bar space is sort of cramped but there are games in the very spacious brewery area. The drinks are delicious and affordable. There are multiple stories that can accomodate a decent crowd. There is a fantastic view of uptown Charlotte from the roof patio. Very sociable atmosphere. Great place to come get a drink with some friends.

Directions

Wooden Robot

Wooden Robot Brewery
Located on South Tryon Street. The establishment itself is small, but the beer is nice. Its' popularity makes it more cramped because there is always an abundance of people. Extensive menu to go along with the drinks. The patio is very small and often times too cramped to stand outside. A wonderful place that needs an expansion because of its' popularity.
