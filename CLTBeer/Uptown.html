Uptown Charlotte



Rock Bottom Brewery
Located on North Tryon Street. Serves great house beers as well as upscale pub food. Was not as crowded as other spots and a severely underrated location. The service is alright, but the selection of beer makes up for any isolated mishaps. A nice atmosphere that anyone can anjoy. A perfect little date spot as well as a relaxing place to have a couple beers. They seemed to have a lot of regulars, so they are obviously doing something right!
